# Privacy Policy — AI Spark for LinkedIn

Last updated: 2026

## What the extension does

When you click the button on a LinkedIn post, the extension reads that post's
visible text and sends it to the AI provider you selected in Settings, to generate
an explanation and optional fact-check. That is the only data that leaves your
browser, and only when you click.

## What is stored, and where

- Your provider choice, optional model, and API key(s) are stored in your browser's
  local extension storage (`storage.local`) on your device only.
- There is no backend server. Nothing is sent to the developer.

## What is sent to third parties

- The post text and your API key are sent directly to the provider you choose
  (Gemini, OpenRouter, Groq, OpenAI, Anthropic, or Pollinations) to generate the
  result. Their handling of that request is governed by their own terms and privacy
  policy.
- Nothing is sent until you click the button.

## What is not collected

- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry.
- The extension is read-only. It does not post, comment, like, message, or modify
  anything on LinkedIn, and it does not read your profile, connections, or messages.

## Your control

- Remove your key any time in Settings, or uninstall the extension to clear all
  stored data.
- Turn the feature off from the toolbar popup.
